Vulpine Creations

At Vulpine Creations discover your creative self. We conduct hobby workshops for Quilling, Candles, Chocolates, Chocolate Bouquets, Origami and Handmade papers - yes! make handmade papers used in various crafts right at your house by recycling paper. At vulpine we not only love to learn and create new things but try to use equipments and materials already present in houses that one might otherwise throw away. Currently we conduct workshops only in Pune, India. If interested please send an email to vulpine.workshop@gmail.com and we will revert :)
